APP NAME: Pro Tracking Fish

SUBTITLE: Smart Aquarium Care & Fish Tracker

DESCRIPTION:
Transform aquarium care into an engaging experience! Track water parameters (temperature, pH, ammonia, nitrates, nitrites) with precision. Your virtual aquarium responds to real data - fish become vibrant with ideal conditions or show stress when parameters need attention. Built-in calculator for water changes, comprehensive statistics, and achievement system. Unlock rare fish species as you progress. Smart notifications for testing and feeding. Perfect for saltwater and freshwater tanks!
